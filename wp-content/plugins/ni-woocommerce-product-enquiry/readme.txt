Ni WooCommerce Product Enquiry plugin allow your web visitor to make the enquiry about a product before purchase.

== Description ==

WooCommerce Product Enquiry plug-in allows your customers or visitors to make an enquiry about a product in product page itself. This plug-in bridges the gap between the potential customer and shop owner.

This Plug-in add an enquiry button on each WooCommerce Product Detail page. By clicking on enquiry button a potential customer can make enquire about product the product before purchasing and by responding or addressing to customers enquiries, you can convert your potentials customers to a buyer.


<h3>Some Features Ni WooCommerce Product Enquiry </h3>

* Fast And Easy Install
* Display Today Enquiry on dashbaord
* Display Total Enquiry on dashbaord
* Email Settings Option (From Name and email address, Subject Line, thank you message)
* Setting option to send enquiry email copy vistior and admin
